What's behind it

A system that runs itself

Alta connects to your CRM, your sales calls, and your existing content to build a real context layer about your business, your buyers, and your competitors. On top of that, it deploys AI agents that carry out the actual work of outbound: research, outreach, replies, and handoff, across real LinkedIn and email identities. LeadStrong configures, trains, and operates it on your behalf, so nothing about running it falls on you.

Always on

Prospecting, outreach, and follow-up run continuously, not in bursts around someone's calendar.

Real identities, not blasts

Every message goes out through a real Rep: a real LinkedIn account and mailbox, never a spray-and-pray sender.

Fully managed

A dedicated LeadStrong team configures, trains, and optimizes the platform, so you're never handed a login and left to figure it out.

Inside the platform

Every capability, built to work together

Katie's Brain

A knowledge base that keeps every message on-brand

Your pitch, your case studies, your sales playbooks, and your positioning all live in one place. Katie references it for every message, so outreach sounds like your business, not a generic template.

  • Pitch decks, case studies, and product pages feed the AI directly
  • Global rules keep tone, terminology, and compliance consistent
  • Refined continuously as campaigns reveal what resonates
Multi-channel touchpoints

LinkedIn and email, working as one sequence

Katie coordinates connection requests, LinkedIn messages, and email into a single sequence per prospect, so nobody gets a duplicate pitch on two channels, and every follow-up has context from what came before.

  • Real LinkedIn Rep accounts, not automation-flagged bots
  • Dedicated warmed mailboxes for deliverable, spam-safe email
  • Each rep's booking link auto-inserted so replies can self-schedule
AI-qualified targeting

Every prospect verified before the first message

Before outreach goes out, AI Conditions check each prospect against your real ICP: company profile, industry fit, disqualifying signals, so budget and volume go toward the accounts that are actually worth pursuing.

  • Filters run before send, not after the fact
  • Broad lists narrowed to genuinely qualified accounts
  • Tuned per campaign as results come in
CRM integration

Clean handoff, no manual entry

Alta connects directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, and other major CRMs, automatically excluding existing customers and competitors, then pushing qualified conversations straight to your sales team the moment they're ready.

  • Existing customers and competitors excluded automatically
  • Qualified replies pushed straight into your pipeline
  • No CRM yet? We'll help you stand up a lightweight one
Co-pilot & Autopilot

You control how hands-on it stays

Every new campaign starts in Co-pilot, with our team reviewing messages before they send. Once quality is proven, campaigns graduate to Autopilot, running independently while we keep monitoring performance behind the scenes.

  • Co-pilot: every message reviewed before it sends
  • Autopilot: fully automated once messaging is validated
  • Performance monitored by our team either way
